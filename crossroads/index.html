I · Which Way Should I Go?

Many paths. Limited light.

Like a traveler at a crossroads, we make daily choices about how to use technology. The Lord has always given His people a way to find the path.

Read each verse and watch for how the Lord guides His people through unfamiliar terrain. What images would you add to a drawing of paths through a forest?

Exodus 13:21–22

"And the Lord went before them by day in a pillar of a cloud, to lead them the way; and by night in a pillar of fire, to give them light; to go by day and night."

Psalm 119:105

"Thy word is a lamp unto my feet, and a light unto my path."

Isaiah 2:3, 5

"…he will teach us of his ways, and we will walk in his paths… O house of Jacob, come ye, and let us walk in the light of the Lord."

A truth
The Lord provides help to guide us in our lives.

III · Where Do I Stand?

A self-quiz on Purpose, Plan, Pause.

Adapted from the Taking Charge of Technology guide. Drag each slider to how true the statement is for you. Be honest — there's no score sent anywhere.

We may sometimes struggle to use technology appropriately, and we may feel guilty when that happens. It's helpful to be aware of influences that encourage harmful technology use — profit-driven companies use tactics designed to foster addictive behaviors if we let them, and excessive use of social media has been shown to trigger depression and other emotional health challenges.

There are things we can do to evaluate our technology use and seek Heavenly Father's help to ensure we are using it appropriately and safely.
