🪵 Lukasa Memory Board Activity

The Luba people used handheld wooden Lukasa boards with colored beads and symbols as tactile "memory maps" to remember history, kings, migrations, and sacred stories. Run your fingers over the beads to recall!

For FHE: Make your own Christ-centered Lukasa to remember the Resurrection — “Christ is risen, truly risen!”

Digital Lukasa Map (click to add beads)

Click anywhere on the board to place extra “remember” beads. Redraw on paper with crayons for family night!
