🪵 Lukasa Memory Board Activity

The ancient Luba (Central Africa) used handheld wooden Lukasa boards with beads and symbols as tactile memory maps. Court historians would trace the beads with their fingers to recall history, genealogy, and sacred stories.

For FHE: Build your own Christ-centered Lukasa to remember the Resurrection — “Christ is risen, truly risen!” Click the board or use buttons to place symbols.
