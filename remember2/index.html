🪵 Lukasa Memory Board Activity

The ancient Luba people (Central Africa) used Lukasa — handheld wooden boards covered in colored beads and symbols — as a "memory map" to remember history, kings, migrations, and sacred stories without writing. Touch the beads to recall!

For FHE: Create your own **Christ-centered Lukasa Treasure Map** on paper or here digitally. Each "bead" symbolizes something to remember about the Savior — especially His Resurrection ("Christ is risen, truly risen!").
